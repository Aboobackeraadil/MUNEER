Our Services

  • AC Repair and Maintenance

    The primary service offered by an AC repair service center is repairing and maintaining air conditioning units. The technicians will diagnose the issue with the AC, replace faulty parts, recharge refrigerant, and ensure that the unit is functioning optimally.

  • AC Installation

    In addition to repairs and maintenance, some AC repair service centers also offer installation services. They will help you choose the right AC unit for your home or office and install it for you.

  • AC Cleaning

    Over time, air conditioning units can accumulate dust, dirt, and other debris that can affect their performance. An AC repair service center may also offer AC cleaning services that involve cleaning the air filters, coils, and other components to ensure that the unit is functioning efficiently.
